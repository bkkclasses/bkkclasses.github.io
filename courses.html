Our UPSC Courses

Structured programs for Prelims, Mains & Interview

SSC Coaching (CGL, CHSL, MTS, GD)

Our SSC coaching program is designed to help aspirants crack examinations such as SSC CGL, CHSL, MTS, and GD with confidence. The course focuses on building strong fundamentals in Quantitative Aptitude, Reasoning, English Language, and General Awareness. Regular mock tests, previous year question analysis, and time-management strategies ensure students develop the accuracy and speed required to succeed in competitive SSC examinations.

Bank Coaching (Clerk & PO)

The Bank Clerk and PO coaching program at BKK Classes prepares students for examinations conducted by IBPS, SBI, and other banking institutions. Our structured approach covers Reasoning Ability, Quantitative Aptitude, English, General Awareness, and Computer Knowledge. Special emphasis is given to sectional timing, exam-oriented practice, and interview preparation to help aspirants secure a career in the banking sector.

MPPSC Coaching

Our MPPSC coaching program is specifically designed for aspirants preparing for the Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission examinations. The course covers complete Prelims and Mains syllabus with a special focus on Madhya Pradesh-specific topics such as history, geography, polity, and current affairs. Regular answer writing practice, state-focused study material, and personalized mentorship help students gain a competitive edge.

UPSC Coaching (IAS, IPS, IFS)

The UPSC coaching program at BKK Classes offers comprehensive preparation for the Civil Services Examination, including Prelims, Mains, and the Interview stage. The curriculum emphasizes conceptual clarity, analytical thinking, current affairs integration, and ethical understanding. With experienced faculty, structured test series, and continuous mentorship, we guide aspirants throughout their journey toward becoming civil servants.
